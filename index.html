A Byte About us

VSSUT InnoBuzz- the second edition of national hackathon organized by VSSUT, Burla under the theme "Ideate, Iterate and Culminate" is an incentive to foster innovative ideas with viability in implementation providing a new impetus to a technical shift in the institution. This hackathon, a flagship event of E-Summit stands firm to the purpose of identifying pivotal issues pertaining to our society and various domains ot organizations. The event will provide a platform to the developers, designers and disruptors across the country to come together and harness their expertise to build products in mealtime and provide effective solution. We aim to take forward the culture set by our Honb'le Prime Minister to promote technologies, student innovations, startups, business incubation and above all crowdsource solutions of problems in public domain across multiple sectors for betterment of nation and set a milestone in the road towards making India a technical superpower. The all-new 36 hours contest would infuse a technological spark within young minds and help them transform the modern era. Gear up to give your intellect a new dimension, a new direction backed by undaunted vigour to dive into a fathomless realm of tech alteration.
